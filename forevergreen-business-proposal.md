**Washington has a crisis.**

The state is facing a severe caregiver shortage. DSHS is desperate for agencies that can deliver reliable, trained caregivers. The demand is real. The need is urgent.

ForeverGreen solves all of this.

## The Business Model

Here's how it works:

**Revenue:** We bill $30/hour for in-home care services to DSHS, Adult Family Homes, and private-pay families.

**Costs:** We pay caregivers $20/hour.

**Margin:** $10/hour before overhead. That's 33% margins—healthy enough to stay sustainable and pay people well.

**Predictability:** Each client averages 30 hours of care per month. That's recurring, predictable revenue.

**Scalability:** As we add more clients, revenue scales without requiring heavy infrastructure or massive capital investment. We're lean by design.

**Diversification:** We contract with DSHS (Medicaid-funded clients), Adult Family Homes, and private-pay families. Multiple revenue streams mean stability.
